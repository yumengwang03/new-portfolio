Find the Mouse!

How to play:

# Step 1: Enter full screen.

# Step 2: Randomly move your mouse in the browser and click once to activate the game (although the mouse will be invisible). Do not touch the mouse again.

# Step 3: In the grid, each square is matched with a key. Press the corresponding key to open the hint for where the mouse is.

# Step 4: You will have 4 tries to find the mouse. Once you find the mouse, it will show up!

# Step 5: Press spacebar to replay.
